Membership Level Benefits

Non-profit (NP) - Free

  • Access to Chamber events and networking opportunities.
  • Inclusion in the Chamber's online directory of non-profit organizations.
  • Access to educational resources and workshops on topics relevant to non-profit management.

Bronze Membership - $100

  • Priority placement in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Access to member-only webinars or seminars on business topics.
  • Promotion of Bronze members in the Chamber's newsletter or social media channels.

Silver - $300

  • Featured listing in the Chamber's online directory with enhanced visibility.
  • Access to Chamber's marketing and advertising resources, such as discounted rates for ad placements on the Chamber's website or newsletter.
  • Priority consideration for sponsorship opportunities at Chamber events.

Gold - $600

  • Premium placement in the Chamber's online directory, with a spotlight position on the homepage.
  • VIP access to Chamber events, including exclusive dinners or receptions with prominent business leaders.
  • Personalized consultation sessions with Chamber staff on business development strategies or advocacy initiatives.
