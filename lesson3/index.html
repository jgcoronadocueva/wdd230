Site Name

The site name will be "Panorama Chamber of Commerce".

Site Purpose

The main purpose of the site "Always Ready" will be to keep the community and visitors informed in matters related to the local weather on the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven located in Idaho State.

This will be accomplished by proviting up to date news about each town, weather tips, images, local activities to enjoy throughout the year, and the forecast for the local areas.
